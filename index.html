About me!

I am a current college student at Duke Kunshan University studying Computation and Design. I'm particularly interested in game design and smart city design, and I aim to combine both, making public spaces and tools more engaging and encourage citizen participation.

I'm originally from Indonesia, but have lived in the US, Saudi Arabia, North Macedonia, and now China. Having the opportunity to experience many different cities in various continents sparked my interest in urban studies. As for games, I have played them since I was 6 when my dad gave me a Game Boy SD that was being given away.

Some fun facts: I have a twin sister, I love the color pink, and one of my favorite things in life is my pet cat, Chonky.
