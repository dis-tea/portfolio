Sustainable DKU Info Hub

Design and Development

Present


I'm working with Sustianable DKU to create an online information platform for sustainable actions, tips, guides, and opportunities. There are two main parts: a website and a WeChat Service program. The website will also serve as the overall website for the Sustainable DKU club. The WeChat Service account is meant to provide easier access to Duke Kunshan Universty (DKU) students, as the super app is an everyday use in China. The goal is to utilize technology to help persuade sustainable behavior on campus.

This project was initiated during my time as a 2023 Millennium Fellow, an initiative for college students to help advance the UN SDGs. You can read more here.

Website

Currently, the website is finished with its lo-fi wireframes, and is in the process of being developed. We designed the overall color scheme using the DKU/Duke website and branding templates, with colors that resemble the Duke image.

WeChat Service

The WeChat Service account is essential in regards to the targeted audience of students at DKU. As the Info Hub's goal is to increase the sustainable actions and information accessibility on campus, it is vital to adopt tools already utilized by the student population. The WeChat Service account will serve as a quick access for links to sustainable shopping, restuarants, guides, opportunities and more.

In the future, we plan to incorporate gamification method of logging and rewarding students with the most recorded sustainable actions to incentive behavior.
