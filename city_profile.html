City Profiles

Solo Project

Nov 2022


In Fall of 2022, I created a City Profile project as part of the Duke Initiative for Urban Studies. I examined three different cities across three different continents: Seattle, Skopje, and Jakarta. I delved into each city's urban life, exploring streetscapes, transportation, and trademark features of the cities. I talked both of my own experience in these cities and online information in an easy to digest way for anyone to read and understand. I designed the pdf with Adobe Express, utilizing the Duke's hue of blue, white, and light lavender accents to create a simple and temperate design that focuses on information.

A City's Flow

The first page of every city's section began with an image of all the city's roads mapped out. This was to preface and examine the urban layout of each city, helping illustrate the flow of city life to readers. The contrast from Seattle's rigid grid style, Skopje's central planning, and Jakarta's maze of streets was also important for me to point out to highlight each city's approach to urban planning.
