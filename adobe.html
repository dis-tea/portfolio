Projects

Spring 2023 | Mar 2024 - Present


In spring of 2023, I created two posters for Int'ly, a former student start-up funded by Duke Kunshan's Innovation Incubator. These posters were for events held by Int'ly to garner more users to the platform as it aimed to be a forum website specifically for international students at DKU and worldwide. These posters were my first exposure to InDesign.

Senior Survery - Int'ly

The Senior Survey poster was for an opportunity for graduates of the class of 2023 at Duke Kunshan to share their tips on job/internship interviews and application processes. Participants were eligible for monetary awards if completed. I aimed for a retro theme for the poster, utilizing orange hue to draw attention to the gift card graphic to incentivize participants.

Free Boba - Int'ly

In early spring, Int'ly held an in-person event where students can register for an account and as a reward, get free bubble tea. I used the a blue-purple hue as the background colors, with white accents and a bubble tea image to focus the attention on the incentive. Large black text with white shadows were furthered used to emphasize the poster's message. Arrow shapes were used to point towards the bubble tea next to the date and the same geometric shape was used throughout for decoration.
