Space Shooters

CS101 Group Project

Feb 2023


I worked on a group project in my Computer Science intro course. We created a two-player pixel shooter game, inspired by the Arcade class, Space Invaders. During the project, I was mainly resposible for designing the game. I created and gathered the assets and coding them into the game. I also designed and created the Main menu, About, and How to pages. We utilized PyGame and I gained my first experience in OOP through this project.

Space Shooters: Demo

The final project as a two-player game where players each control their own ships and shooot at each other until their health drops. The game was made to be two-player to create a more engaging game that can be played with friends.

Space Shooter: Prototype

The first prototype was more similar to our inspiration, Alien Invaders. It involves a one-player versus computer in which the player has to shoot at the incoming aliens and keep advancing to the next stage, which has infinite levels but gets increasingly harder. The aliens themselves shoot at the player. This prototype did not feature a menu and only the game itself.
